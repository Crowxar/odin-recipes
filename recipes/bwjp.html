Bacon Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers

Description

These jalapeño poppers are creamy in the middle with crispy bacon on the outside. They're easy to make and taste so much better than traditional poppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package Cheddar cheese
  • 6 jalapeno peppers, seeded and halved
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Preheated oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Make the filling.
  3. Fill each jalapeño half with cheese mixture, then put the halves back together.
  4. Wrap each reassembled jalapeño with bacon.
  5. Cook in the preheated oven until the bacon is crispy (25-35 minutes)
